What's Included In Your Dry Hire

My All-Inclusive Mobile Bartending Experience

With my Dry Hire service, you supply the alcohol, and I bring the complete bar experience to you. Here’s what you can expect:

  • Professional Bartending by Mary Katherine: Friendly and experienced service for you and your guests.
  • Custom Drink Menu Consultation: We'll collaborate on a curated list of up to 3 Signature Cocktails tailored to your event, plus guidance for beer & wine selections.
  • Detailed Shopping List Assistance: I'll help you figure out exactly what and how much alcohol to purchase, making it cost-effective.
  • Essential Bar Tools & Equipment: Shakers, jiggers, pour spouts, cutting boards, knives, etc.
  • Basic Mixers & Garnishes: Standard juices (orange, cranberry, pineapple), sodas (Coke, Diet Coke, Sprite, club soda, tonic), and fresh garnishes (lemons, limes, oranges, cherries).
  • Premium Options Available: Ask about specialty syrups, fresh herbs, or unique garnishes for an elevated experience (additional cost may apply).
  • Coolers & Ice: I'll bring the coolers and ensure plenty of ice to keep drinks perfectly chilled throughout your event.
  • Disposable Drinkware, Napkins & Straws: Quality disposable options are included. Upgraded options available upon request.
  • Complete Setup & Teardown: I handle all the bar-related setup before your event and cleanup afterward.
  • Travel: Includes travel up to 30 miles from my home base (an additional fee applies for further distances).
  • Service Duration: Up to 4 hours of active bartending service (additional hours can be arranged).
  • Insurance: General & Liquor Liability Insurance for peace of mind.

If you opt not to have a tip jar, a 20% service charge will be added to your total bill.
