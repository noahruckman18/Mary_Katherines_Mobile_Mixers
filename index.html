About Me

Hi! I am Mary Katherine. I have been a bartender for the last 7 years. I got started when I worked a corporate serving/bartending job and fell in love with being able to provide a fun atmosphere while putting together new and unique drinks. I love being able to meet new people every day and also building relationships with new and already existing customers. I pride myself on my work while also practicing RAS (Responsible Alcohol Service). Throughout my years in the service industry, I have imporved my bartending skills and am confident in myt ability to craft a spceialized drink list for your special occasion. I look forward to meeting you and providing my services for your event! :)
