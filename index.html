Meet Mary Katherine

Hi! I'm Mary Katherine, a bartender with 7 years of experience. I started in a corporate bartending job and fell in love with creating unique drinks and providing a fun atmosphere. I enjoy meeting new people and building relationships with customers.

I pride myself on practicing Responsible Alcohol Service (RAS). Over the years, I've improved my skills and am confident in crafting a specialized drink list for your special occasion. I look forward to meeting you and serving at your event! :)
